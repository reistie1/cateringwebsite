About Us

Wendy Gottmers

Head Chef/Owner

My name is Wendy and I am the head chef at Southern Belles Catering. I over 20 years of cooking experience in the catering industry, catering events from tea parties, personal and corporate events, and weddings ranging in size. My cooking inspiration comes from my time in Italy where I was classically trained in tradition italian cooking, which has been the influence for a variety of dishes we offer. I was also the Banquet Manager at Victoria Golf Course for many years and understand how important great food no matter the event, people remember good food as much as they remember the event itself. I work closely with my customers to ensure that the food meets the highest quality standards and customer satisfaction with our services provided.
